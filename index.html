Service Management System

Version 1.9.6 — Tester Build

Modern scheduling software for window cleaning companies

Replaces legacy FoxPro software. Runs locally on Mac or Windows — no internet required.

What is SMS?
Everything you need to run a window cleaning operation — customers, jobs, orders, billing, and weekly procedures in one place.

Customers & Jobs

Manage your full customer list with billing settings, contact info, recurring job schedules, and service history.

Orders & Invoicing

Track daily work orders, record payments, mark missed or cancelled jobs, and generate invoices with one click.

Reports & Tools

Weekly summaries, 90-day A/R aging, sub-contractor payments, and a guided Sunday workflow for batch rescheduling.
